How we'll use this

1. Upload HH after you play

Any ACR/WSOP session gets added to the raw hand history folder, parsed, and rolled into your stats.

2. Review coolers vs real mistakes

We'll separate unavoidable variance from actual leaks so study time goes where it matters.

3. Keep a running study loop

Each session updates your biggest leaks, best hands, and next daily drill focus.
